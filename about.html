About Campus Lost & Found

Helping our community reunite lost items with their owners.

Our Mission

Campus Lost & Found is a college-focused platform designed to help students, faculty, and staff quickly recover or report lost items around campus.

What We Do

Whether it's a misplaced ID card, mobile phone, wallet, or even books, our system allows users to post, search, and claim lost items securely and efficiently.

How It Works

Users can easily report lost items by filling out a simple form. Found items can also be submitted by anyone who discovers them. Our platform matches lost items with found reports, making it easier for users to reclaim their belongings.

Building Community

This system builds a trustworthy environment within the college, promoting responsibility and cooperation among the campus community.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team consists of students and faculty members who are passionate about creating a supportive campus environment. We work together to ensure that lost items are returned to their rightful owners.
